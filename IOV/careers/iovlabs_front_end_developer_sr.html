Do you want to join the Internet of Value revolution? We want to hear from you!
Based in LATAM Front End Developer Sr Full time

Taringa! is the top Spanish-speaking social platform in the world with 30 million registered users and over 1,000 active online communities. Taringa! allows users to share their interests in topic-based channels where they can create long and short form posts with rich text, images, video and more.

We are looking for a Front end developer to join our team and work on our Taringa! Platform.

Responsibilities:

  • Work in multidisciplinary team with other professionals such as back-end developers
  • Get feedback from users and customers
  • Make suggestions for better solutions to problems
  • Optimize application for maximum speed and scalability
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs
  • Deliver content suitable for open source collaboration
  • Ensure reliability by elaborating end to end testing

Skills, Knowledge & Abilities:

  • Bachelor or advanced student of Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Systems or related careers
  • Written and verbal English proficiency
  • Knowledge in
    • CSS (SaaS, Less or any other pre processor will be a plus)
    • ReactJS (exclusive)
    • Redux
    • Node.js (will be a plus)
    • Experience integrating frontend with external services calling APIs (like RESTful services)
    • Jest or other testing frameworks
    • We would like to see some previous work on ReactJS and Redux
  • Great interpersonal and communication skills
  • Good time-management skills

Location

  • Based in LATAM

If you are interested to be part of our technological revolution, we invite you to contact us so we can provide you with more information about our company and the opportunity to work with us. Send your CV at talent@iovlabs.org
