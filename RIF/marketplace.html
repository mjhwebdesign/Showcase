RIF Marketplace

The RIF Marketplace (MP) is where service providers connect with their potential users. It will be used as a building block for the exchange of services in the Decentralized Sharing Economies (DSE) built on top of RSK and RIF. It will also be the place to go in order to consume RIF services such as persistent storage (file pinning service), domain exchange service for RNS, or a “watchtower” service for RIF Payments. With more to be added as they are released.

RIF Marketplace will include a set of smart contracts indexing the offers on the RSK blockchain, a marketplace library which allows direct access for the dapp developers and finally a friendly user interface for the non-technical customers.
