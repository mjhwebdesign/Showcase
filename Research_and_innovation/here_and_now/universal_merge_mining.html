Universal Merge Mining
“Besides the extra security for other blockchains, this project positions RSK as the point of contact between said blockchains and Bitcoin security. By doing the former, UMM leverages the ecosystem and contributes to Bitcoin sustainability by bringing more revenue to its miners.”
martin medina & Sergio Lerner
5 min read

The goal of this project is to provide a way for other blockchains to be secured through RSK merged mined hashing power.

The main challenges of Universal Merged Mining are:

  • Extend RSK blockchain design to accept other chains information and relay it to the Bitcoin network in order to obtain PoW.
  • Design a set of smart-contracts to obtain, provide and query all the UMM information from RSK network.
  • Create a proposal that is simple and suits as many blockchains as possible in order to maximize adoption.

Besides the extra security for other blockchains, this project positions RSK as the point of contact between said blockchains and Bitcoin security. By doing the former, UMM leverages the ecosystem and contributes to Bitcoin sustainability by bringing more revenue to its miners.

UMM is lots of fun because it has a bit of everything: research, software design, coding, written improvement proposals and having adoption meetings with other blockchains.
