Alejandro Narancio
RIF Strategist Connect

Alejandro has over 15 years of experience in the technology industry working as software Solution Architect focused in distributed systems and innovative technologies. He has worked at top organizations in the in IT business such as TataTechnologies Wiki You and Tivit. His areas of expertise include Web architecture and development, system refactoring, cloud computing, client and project management.

He founded Infuy 13 years ago a software development company based on Uruguay specialized on outsourcing services of web & mobile applications in java, php, clojure, reactJS, reactNative, nodejs for worldwide clients. Infuy has focused its development since 2015 on blockchain technologies (RSK, Ethereum, among others). He joined IOVlabs first as Product Leader for RIF Lumino Network and later became RIF Strategist.
